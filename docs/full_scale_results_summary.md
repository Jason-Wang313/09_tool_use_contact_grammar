# Full-Scale Results Summary

## Main Result

In the baseline full-scale contact world, contact grammar reaches F1 1.000 with false-positive rate 0.000. Unordered contacts reach F1 0.702 with false-positive rate 0.347, and static affordances reach F1 0.633 with false-positive rate 0.473.

## Noise Boundary

At 10% independent predicate noise, direct grammar has F1 0.861; targeted probe fallback reaches F1 1.000 with mean probes 3.17.

## Rule Falsification

Dropping the side gate produces false-positive rate 0.084. Coarsening away all roles gives F1 0.917.

## Counterexamples

In blocked-side counterexamples, unordered contacts have false-positive rate 1.000, while the fine grammar rejects the invalid traces.
